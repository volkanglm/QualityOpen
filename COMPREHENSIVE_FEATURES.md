# 🇬🇧 ENGLISH (EN)

### 📁 1. Project & Data Management
- **Universal Import**: Support for TXT, DOCX, PDF, Video, and Image files.
- **Project Styles**: Choose between "Primary Research" or "Meta-Synthesis (QMARS)" workflows.
- **Multilingual Support**: Fully localized interface in 8 languages.
- **Smart Explorer**: Organize documents with custom colors, tags, and types (Interview, Field Note, etc.).
- **Local Database**: High-performance IndexedDB storage for instant data retrieval.

### 🧘 2. Multimodal Coding & Interface
- **Pixel-Perfect Coding**: Unified workspace for text highlighting and media (Video/Image) segmentation.
- **Dynamic Code Tree**: Unlimited hierarchy with drag-and-drop organization and vibrant color coding.
- **Keyboard-First Design**: CMD+K Command Palette and 'C' shortcuts for ultra-fast workflows.
- **Split-View & Sync**: Compare documents side-by-side with synchronized scrolling.
- **Line Numbering**: Scientific precision with toggleable line numbers for citations.

### 📊 3. Visual Analytics & Dashboard
- **Thematic Networks**: Interactive 2D/3D maps including Standard, Solar System, and Anchor Node views.
- **Code Matrix**: Heatmap visualization of code frequencies across documents.
- **Narrative Flow**: Chronological distribution of themes across your data.
- **Typology Mapping**: Automatic clustering of cases based on thematic intensity.
- **DNA Map & Portrait**: Sequential visualization of coding patterns within a single document.
- **Code Cloud**: Dynamic typographic hierarchy of your most used concepts.

### 🧠 4. AI-Assisted Intelligence (BYOK)
- **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)**: Support for OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude 3.5), and Google (Gemini).
- **AI Thematic Coding**: Automatically extract themes and codes from research questions.
- **Synthesis Engine**: Generate QMARS-compliant meta-syntheses and cross-tab summaries.
- **Chat with Documents**: Ask questions directly to your PDFs and interviews with full context awareness.
- **Smart Summarization**: Instant AI insights for newly imported documents.

### 🎓 5. Scientific Rigor & JARS Compliance
- **Reflexivity Journal**: Dedicated, distraction-free Markdown editor to document researcher bias.
- **Immutable Audit Trail**: Automatic logging of every code creation, modification, and deletion.
- **Disconfirming Evidence**: "Zap" icon to mark and justify outliers (İstisna Avcısı).
- **Saturation Radar**: Visual proof of theoretical saturation—know exactly when to stop data collection.
- **Protocol Versioning**: Track the evolution of your research protocol with a full history and change logs.
- **JARS Checklist**: Built-in APA JARS compliance tracker to ensure high-quality reporting.

### 📤 6. Export & Reporting
- **Scholarly Word Export**: Generate .docx reports with APA-style citations and automatic reference lists.
- **Interactive HTML Reports**: Share your entire analysis as a standalone, beautiful web package.
- **Data Tables**: Export segments as Excel/CSV for further statistical processing.
- **Member Checking Export**: Specialized report for participant validation, grouped by theme.
- **Visual Export**: Save charts and networks as high-resolution PNG or JPEG.

### 🛡️ 7. Security, Sync & System
- **100% Offline Power**: Work without internet; your data never leaves your device by default.
- **Lifetime License**: One-time payment ($149), no subscriptions, forever yours.
- **Privacy-First Sync**: Optional encrypted backup to your own Google Drive.
- **Local Folder Sync**: Premium feature to mirror your project data to a local directory for redundant backup.
- **Hardware-Locked Security**: Military-grade local encryption tied to your device.
